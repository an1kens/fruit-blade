Design direction

Mood: "Candy arcade at night" — playful saturated foreground (fruits, accents) on calm lavender-night backdrop. Webcam feed is part of the brand but always behind scrim on menus. One accent (#ffe566) for selected/important. Gesture targets ≥88px sprites, ≥140px center spacing. Fredoka display + Nunito Sans UI.
